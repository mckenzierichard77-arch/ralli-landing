Our promise

What you'll never find here.

Your community, your feed.

What you see is what people you actually follow are using, loving, or breaking out from. No influencer algorithm. No engagement bait. Just your circle, sorted by what's new.

No paid placements.

Brands cannot pay to appear, rank higher, or be featured. Our editorial picks are exactly that — editorial. Your feed has no native ads, no boosted posts, no sponsored "you might like" cards.

Your data stays yours.

We don't sell your data. Ever. Your routine and personal lists are private by default — only what you explicitly post is visible to others. Read our privacy policy →

Honest by design.

Every product score is calculated from its actual ingredient list, not from how much the brand spent on marketing. We show you the receipts — including ingredients we flag and why.

Our story

Built out of necessity.

Ralli was born from the same frustration we know you've felt — spending hours deciphering ingredient labels, second-guessing whether a glowing review was paid for, and never quite trusting that the "dermatologist recommended" label meant anything real.

As sisters, we kept sending each other screenshots asking "is this pore-clogging?" We realized our most trusted source was each other — not ads, not influencers. So we built the tool we always wished existed.
