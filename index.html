What it is

Built for ingredient-conscious skin.

Every product you scan gets an honest pore score from a database of 250+ ingredients — paired with reactions from people with skin like yours. The way skincare research should have always worked.

Every ingredient in a product is matched against a curated comedogenic database, then averaged into a single 0–5 score. Green for clear. Red for caution.
